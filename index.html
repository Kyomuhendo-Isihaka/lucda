About Us

Who Are We?

LUCDA is a student’s association focused on forming local solutions for global problems, we believe that solutions to all our problems are within the people’s heads affected by the problem, they only need to be mentored on how to solve the problems through capacity building, ensuring awareness and exposure to resources required to solve the problem in place.
